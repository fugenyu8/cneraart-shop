## 三阶段启蕴仪式描述

### 中文
1. **净化启蕴** - 以传统香料、净水和古老吟诵进行净化，唤醒器物的文化能量
2. **传承吟诵** - 文化传承人以虔诚之心进行传统吟诵，注入千年文化智慧
3. **能量赋予** - 融合五台山千年文化底蕴，赋予每件饰品独特的守护能量

### 英文
1. **Purification & Awakening** - Purified with traditional incense, spring water, and ancient chants to awaken the artifact's cultural energy
2. **Heritage Chanting** - Cultural lineage holders perform traditional chanting with deep devotion, infusing millennia of cultural wisdom
3. **Energy Empowerment** - Drawing upon Mount Wutai's thousand-year cultural heritage, each piece receives unique guardian energy
